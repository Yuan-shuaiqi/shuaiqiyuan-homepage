About Me

I am committed to developing quantitative models for assessing the reliability, risk, and resilience of engineering systems, focusing on ensuring safety and security. My research encompasses industrial cyber-physical systems, chemical process safety, dynamic risk assessment, fault diagnosis, uncertainty quantification in machine learning, and trustworthy AI for safety-critical applications.

I have authored approximately 30 peer-reviewed publications and contributed several book chapters in the fields of process safety and security. For a comprehensive overview of my work, please visit my Google Scholar profile. Additionally, I actively contribute to the academic community as a reviewer for over 20 international journals, including Safety Science and Reliability Engineering & System Safety.
