Shuaiqi Yuan (原帅琪)

I am dedicated to developing quantitative models for reliability, risk, and resilience assessment of engineering systems. My expertise includes industrial cyber-physical systems, chemical process safety, dynamic risk assessment, fault diagnosis, uncertainty quantification, and reliability assurance of AI in safety-critical settings.

I have published around thirty peer-reviewed papers and several book chapters in process safety and security. You can explore my full publication list on Google Scholar. I also serve as a reviewer for over twenty international journals, including Safety Science and Reliability Engineering & System Safety.
